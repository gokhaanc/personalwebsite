Common Questions

How do I restore my subscription?
Open the app, go to Settings, tap "Unlock All Themes", then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the paywall.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Imposto, then tap "Cancel Subscription".

The app crashed or something isn't working.
Please email us at hi@gokhan.pw with a description of the issue and your device model. We'll look into it.
